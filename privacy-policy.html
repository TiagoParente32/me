Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Homify respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address
  • Name and profile information
  • Authentication identifiers

Usage & App Data

  • App activity and interactions
  • Associated data sxuch as memberships or shared content

Push Notifications

  • Device push notification token (FCM token)

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To authenticate users securely
  • To provide app features and functionality
  • To send important notifications
  • To improve app performance and reliability

Data Storage & Security

We use Google Firebase services, including Firebase Authentication and Firestore, to securely store and manage user data. Reasonable technical measures are used to protect your information.

Third-Party Services

This app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Cloud Firestore
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM)

Account Deletion

You can delete your account directly from within the app:

  1. Open the app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Select "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm deletion

When you delete your account:

  • Your authentication account is permanently removed
  • Your user profile and associated data are deleted
  • Notification tokens are removed

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Data deletion occurs immediately or within 24 hours.

Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
