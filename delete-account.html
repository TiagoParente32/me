Account Deletion

Users can delete their account directly from the app.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Select Delete Account
  4. Confirm deletion

What Data Is Deleted

  • Authentication account
  • User profile and related data stored in Firestore
  • Associated memberships, houses, and notification tokens

Data Retention

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All data is deleted immediately or within 24 hours.
